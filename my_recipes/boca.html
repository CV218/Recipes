Boca Burger

Ingredients

  • Boca Patty
  • Hamburger Bun
  • Avocado
  • Cheddar
  • Mayonaise

Instructions

  1. Cook Boca patty in skillet on medium high heat with a small amount of oil
  2. Flip Boca patty after it is crispy and add cheese to top of crispy side
  3. Toast bun and mash avocado with salt, pepper, and garlic
  4. Assemble with mayo on top bun and avocado on bottom bun
