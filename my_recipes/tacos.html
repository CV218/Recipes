Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Tortillas
  • lime
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into 3/4in cubes and sear in skillet, adding spices and lime juice
  2. Fry tortillas in a well oiled pan
  3. Assemble with chicken, lime juice, pico, and sour cream
