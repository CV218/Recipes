Chorizo, Egg, and Sausage

Ingredients

  • Aidell's Chicken Apple Sausage
  • 2 eggs
  • 2oz Chorizo
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Fry Chorizo until rendered, then add chopped chicken sausage
  2. Once chicken sausage is browned, and beaten eggs and stir
  3. After egg is formed, plate and top with salsa
